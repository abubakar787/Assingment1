Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is a three-day conference for software developers, engineers, and tech professionals featuring 48+ talks, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Where and when is the event?

The event takes place from October 2-4, 2026, at the San Francisco Convention Center. Physical tickets are sold out, but virtual attendance is still available.

What is the refund policy?

You can get a full refund for cancellations made 30 days before the event. Partial refunds are available up to 14 days before. After that, tickets are non-refundable.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your laptop, charger, business cards, and comfortable shoes. We also recommend bringing a reusable water bottle to reduce waste.

Is the event accessible?

Yes, the venue is fully ADA accessible. Please contact our support team if you have specific accommodation needs.
